Glad to see you here, welcome to my blog. This blog is all about my journey to web Development. It is a long story before I came up to this career, from construction worker, promo dicer, pool watcher, warehouse crew, up to manufacturing technician. I experienced a lot of things and those experiences teaches me a lesson in life. I know I am far away to my desired destination, but I know one day I will reach those place that I wanted to be. It reminds me of my father once told me "It doesn’t matter if you do it slow, as long as your determination and proactive desire still remain and never stop".

Rainy season of 2022 when I quit my job as a Senior Test Technician in a Semiconductor Manufacturing Industry. It is eight years of solid technical experience the longest job that I ever committed. I love the job before because it is full of challenge and I am curious about my job, the automation testing, the electromechanical concept that applied to the real world. I am happy fixing and exploring those things and my curiosity satisfied. Years goes by and I never imagined it is almost a decade and I feel the torch of my determination slowly fading. Then I realize that I am stuck into my comfort zone, doing things that I already knew and its bore me and I want change.

Then I meet KodeGO, an organization offering a bootcamp and teach how to code and to develop websites. I do research about web development, the nature of their job, what are they doing then I found it interesting.
